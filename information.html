About Our Facility

Patriot Arena is a state-of-the-art multipurpose venue designed to host a wide variety of events, from sporting competitions to concerts, trade shows, and community gatherings.

Our facility features a 10,000-seat main arena, multiple conference rooms, exhibition halls, and modern amenities to ensure an exceptional experience for all guests.

Arena Features:

  • 10,000-seat main arena with state-of-the-art acoustics
  • Multiple conference and meeting rooms
  • 20,000 sq ft exhibition hall
  • Full-service concessions and catering options
  • Free parking for over 3,000 vehicles

Arena Rules & Guidelines

General Rules

  • All guests must have a valid ticket for entry
  • No outside food or beverages allowed
  • Smoking is prohibited in all indoor areas
  • Weapons of any kind are strictly prohibited

Prohibited Items

  • Weapons, firearms, knives, or items that may be used as weapons
  • Outside food and beverages
  • Illegal substances
  • Professional cameras or recording equipment (without proper credentials)

Accessibility Services

Patriot Arena is committed to providing an accessible environment for all guests. We offer a variety of services to ensure everyone can enjoy our events.

Accessible Seating

Accessible seating is available in all sections of the arena. Companions may sit with guests who require accessible seating. To purchase accessible seating tickets, please contact our box office at (555) 123-4567.

Parking

Accessible parking spaces are available in all parking lots. These spaces are located close to the entrances and are clearly marked. A valid accessible parking permit must be displayed.

Additional Services

  • Assistive listening devices
  • Sign language interpretation (with advance notice)
  • Service animals are welcome

Contact for Accessibility Needs

If you have specific accessibility requirements or questions, please contact our Guest Services team at (555) 123-4567 or email accessibility@patriotarena.com.
