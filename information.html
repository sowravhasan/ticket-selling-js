Seating Charts & Layout

Explore our comprehensive seating chart to find the perfect view for your event. Our arena features multiple seating sections designed to provide exceptional sightlines and comfort for every guest.

From premium courtside seats to budget-friendly upper-level options, we offer a variety of seating choices to fit every preference and budget.

Seating Sections:

  • Premium Courtside (Sections A1-A4) - $400-500 per seat
  • Lower Level (Sections 101-118) - $250-350 per seat
  • Upper Level (Sections 201-230) - $150-200 per seat
  • General Admission (Sections 301-340) - $85-120 per seat
  • Accessible seating available in all levels with companion seats

Arena Policies

General Rules

  • All guests must have a valid ticket for entry
  • No outside food or beverages allowed
  • Smoking is prohibited in all indoor areas
  • Weapons of any kind are strictly prohibited

Prohibited Items

  • Weapons, firearms, knives, or items that may be used as weapons
  • Outside food and beverages
  • Illegal substances
  • Professional cameras or recording equipment (without proper credentials)

Arena Amenities

Patriot Arena offers a wide range of amenities to enhance your event experience. From dining options to comfortable facilities, we ensure every guest has access to premium services.

Dining & Concessions

  • Multiple concession stands throughout the arena
  • Premium dining options in VIP lounges
  • Local craft beer and specialty beverages

Facilities & Services

  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the venue
  • Climate-controlled environment
  • ATM machines and mobile payment options
  • Family restrooms and baby changing stations

Accessibility Features

  • Assistive listening devices available
  • Accessible seating in all sections
  • Accessible parking spaces available
